We’re defining digital identity
for health

Identity is everything in today’s world. Are you who you claim you are? Current identity systems - archaic, insecure and forgeable - can’t reliably answer this question. This is what we’re tackling at Immunodex.


We’re starting with what matters most - health. Governments should safeguard the health of their citizens; hospitals, their staff and patients; and businesses, their employees and customers. These institutions need verifiably healthy people to function.


Our mission is to provide everyone with a trusted, health-based identity, establishing a global healthtech network that any institution can rely on for making and enforcing strategy and policy.
